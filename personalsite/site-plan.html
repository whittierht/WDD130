Purpose

Many websites are used to sell a product, but I believe that a website will only sell a product if it demonstrates the product in such a way that the website sells itself first. I want my website to sell Video Games, but I want the website to first sell itself before it sells the product. Doesn’t it make sense that if the website doesn’t first sell itself, then how will it sell its product?

Content

Home page

Why people will visit our site

People will come to my site to save money on their favorite video games. Our company will buy videos games when they are on discount for a limited time, and then when the price goes back to normal we sell the games at the discounted price with a little fee for profit, but it will still be less than it was before. This is how we will help people save money while still making a profit! I also want people from all consoles to use the site!

Why people will trust us

The homepage will also state our purpose and how we help people save money. Doing a concrete explanation on how we are able to save people money will help our potential customers know that we are not a scam company. We will also put our social medias and customer service number for people who want to look us up more.

How it will look and feel

The the homepage will have links to all of the different pages! There will be 3 pages, one for the Home Page, one for Video Games, and one for Puchases. By covering each console, more people will use our site just because of its diversity. Each link will be a picture o button that says something like "Save Now" or "Purchace" This will bring them to the respective page. There will also be links to each of these pages in the header.

Since the website is meant specifically for those who play video games, the age range will most likley be 14-23 years old males. This website will be specifically tailored to appeal to video gamers. With a video games feel for the design. Not only will there be pictures of video games, but I also want the website to be very interactive since video games themselves are active. When the customer hovers over a button, there will be some sort of transition and color change. I don't want these transitions to be immediate so they will most likley have a duration of less than half a second.

I want to find an image that will work for the whole background, I'm not the biggest fan of the white background on most sites. The image will need to be simple, but also make it feel like the webiste is alive. It will probably be prominatly white with some video game references.

Other Ideas

In a website like this, people will be buying things. I will most likley have a button by each video game that says "Buy Now and Save." This will bring them to the third page which is the purchace Page
